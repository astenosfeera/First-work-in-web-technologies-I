Cyprus

I decided to go to Cyprus on my birthday which is on 29.11. Found a cheap flights to Cyprus. "That's it" I thought. It is around +24 degrees still and I want to feel like in summer. A bit more sun, a bit more light. I stayed in Paphos for 2 nights, in mountain village near mount. Olympus for 2 nights and then near Larnaca - you guessed it correctly - again for 2 nights. On Paphos side I visited canyon and hiked there but the storm with rain and thunder started. It was adventurous! After I visited a half-drown ship. Then I visited mountain and hiked around Olympus mountain. And the final days I spent in Larnaca and went to sea caves, Ayia Napa beach and met 2 girls from South America. :] (this actually happened in 2025 but other things are written in 1.course)


Reviews

X-files

I've decided to rewatch all seasons of X-files. I actually never watched all seasons but just some random episodes when I was a kid. Currently I've watched 14 episodes of first season. I can tell it's a bit old - 90s fashion and the quality of making special effects is lower than nowadays. But it still has its charm. I have a calming feeling when I watch it. Some episodes feel like fillers. I think the one I liked the least was "Space". There are patterns and similar structure to some episodes. For example there was a guy who could control fire and in another serie there was a guy who could deform his body to move in shafts and then to kill people. Same motives but different superpower so to say. But I really liked one of the first episodes about alien technologies - not really technologies but the atmosphere and Fox Mulder seeing an alien space ship. :D It was funny but nice. Another of my favorites was Squeeze - the one I already mentioned - guy deforming in shafts - it was a bit scary. Another one - Scully finally saw something extraordinary was in "Beyond the Sea" and the next one "Gender Bender". Those two also were my favorites.
